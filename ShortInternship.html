During my short 10 week internship at S.P. Abonneeservice, I had the opportunity to work on improving the online forms, the subscription overview page, and the contact page for two publishers: OOR and NIW.

The goal of the project was to simplify and accelerate the subscription process for users while creating a more user-friendly and intuitive experience. My tasks included modifying forms in Quartz, designing updated web pages in Photoshop, and implementing responsive prototypes using Bootstrap.

Development Progress

Initial Design

The initial design phase focused on exploring ways to streamline the user experience. For the forms, I analyzed the existing structure to identify areas where steps could be reduced or unnecessary fields removed.

For the subscription overview page, I aimed to create a clear and organized layout that simplified the process of selecting an appropriate subscription. On the contact page, I added visual elements like icons to improve readability and navigation.

I created wireframes and design mockups in Photoshop during this stage, drawing inspiration from successful elements in previous S.P. Abonneeservice projects.

Development

After finalizing the initial designs, I began the development phase. Using Quartz, I modified the online forms to make them more compact and efficient.

For the subscription overview and contact pages, I used Photoshop to refine the visual elements and Bootstrap to implement responsive, functional prototypes. This allowed the pages to be accessible on a variety of devices.

Testing was a key part of this phase, as I created multiple versions of the designs and adjusted them based on feedback from my supervisor and the publishers. These prototypes were aimed at reducing user friction while maintaining a clean and professional appearance.

Results

The final deliverables included:

  • Improved structure of the subscription overview page
  • Enhanced contact page with icons and better layout
  • Received positive feedback from supervisor and publishers

Reflections

During this internship, I improved my communication and collaboration skills by working closely with clients and gathering feedback throughout the process. I also strengthened my ability to explain and justify design decisions, especially when presenting to publishers. Overall, it deepened my understanding of user-centered design and boosted my technical confidence.
